Our Heritage

Born in Makhado

CRBFTN is a professional fashion brand born from the vibrant streets of Makhado, Limpopo, where tradition meets innovation. We specialize in premium clothing, trendy youth fashion, and professional accessories.

Our diverse collection includes premium streetwear for the urban culture enthusiasts, cool trendy fashion designed specifically for youth who want that perfect drip, and formal wear including golf shirts and professional T-shirts for the modern professional.

Beyond clothing, we craft premium accessories and bags - from stylish backpacks to professional laptop bags and trendy side bags. Every piece tells a story of quality craftsmanship and cutting-edge style.

Whether you're looking for street culture vibes, professional elegance, or youth trendy fashion - CRBFTN delivers premium quality that speaks your language and matches your lifestyle.
