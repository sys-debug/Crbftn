Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer free nationwide shipping on orders over R500. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days, express delivery 1-2 business days.

What's your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns on unworn items with tags attached. Return shipping is free for exchanges, R50 for refunds.

Do you offer custom sizing?

Yes! We offer custom sizing for select items. Contact us with your measurements and we'll create a piece just for you.

Are your products ethically made?

Absolutely. All our products are made in our Makhado studio with fair wages and sustainable practices. We're committed to ethical fashion.
