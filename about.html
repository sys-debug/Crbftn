The Beginning

CRBFTN was born from a simple idea: creating premium clothing that celebrates South African culture while meeting international standards. Founded in Makhado, Limpopo, we started with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern streetwear.

Our founders, passionate about both fashion and heritage, saw an opportunity to showcase the rich textile traditions of Limpopo to the world through contemporary designs.
